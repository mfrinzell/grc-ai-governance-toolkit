# GRC & AI Governance Toolkit

> A practitioner-built collection of templates, frameworks, and tools for Governance, Risk & Compliance programs — with a focus on AI governance, executive reporting, and cross-functional accountability.

---

## About This Repo

This toolkit was built from hands-on experience leading GRC, AI governance, fraud detection, and data governance programs across enterprise environments. These are working templates — not academic exercises — designed for program managers and compliance leads who need to move fast and communicate clearly with stakeholders at every level.

**Core competencies reflected here:**
- AI Governance & Responsible AI Frameworks
- Enterprise Risk Management & Risk Register Design
- RACI / DACI Accountability Modeling
- Executive & Board-Level Reporting
- Vendor & Third-Party Risk Assessment
- Program Health Scorecards & KRI/KPI Dashboards

## How to Use These Templates

1. Each file is a standalone Markdown template — copy, adapt, and make it your own
2. Templates are designed to be tool-agnostic (compatible with Confluence, Notion, SharePoint, Google Docs)
3. Scoring rubrics use a consistent 1–5 scale throughout for easy cross-program comparison

## Frameworks Referenced

These templates align with or draw from:
- **NIST AI RMF** (AI Risk Management Framework)

- **SOC 2 Type II** control categories
- **COSO ERM** Framework
